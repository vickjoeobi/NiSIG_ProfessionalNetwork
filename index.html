An exclusive network for Nigerian career professionals in Germany. This network is targeted at former students who are gainfully employed or currently transiting from student to full-time professionals. Students who consider themselves professionals are also welcome to join. Potential members of this network also include Ph.D./PostDoc candidates in Germany. Non-member of NiSIG who is employed in Germany is also welcome to join.

"NiSIG Professional Network is an initiative of the Board of Trustees of the Nigerian Scholars in Germany with the sole aim of creating an impactful synergy between Nigerian scholars, intellectuals, and professionals in Germany."


Why Join NiSIG Professionals
